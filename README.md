# 📊 Employee Attrition Analysis

## 🧩 Business Problem
Employee attrition is a major challenge affecting organizational stability and increasing recruitment costs. This project analyzes workforce data to identify key drivers of employee turnover and improve retention strategies.

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Employee demographics (age, gender)
- Department and job role
- Tenure
- Salary
- Performance and job satisfaction
- Attrition status and reasons

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard and visualization)
- Excel / SQL (Data preparation and analysis)

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate is ~19%, indicating moderate turnover
- Mid-tenure employees show the highest attrition levels
- Attrition varies significantly across departments and branches
- Lower job satisfaction and performance levels are linked to higher attrition
- Key attrition reasons include retirement, career growth limitations, and personal factors

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Improve employee engagement and career development programs
- Focus retention strategies on mid-tenure employees
- Review compensation and growth opportunities
- Address department-specific attrition issues

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis provides actionable insights to help organizations reduce employee turnover and improve workforce stability.
